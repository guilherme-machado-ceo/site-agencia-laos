About Laos Agency

We are a full-service agency focused on branding, content and multicultural marketing. We work with local impact and global positioning projects, with symbolic depth, refined aesthetics and strategic clarity.

The agency was founded by Rebeca and Guilherme, a couple passionate about languages, cultures and technologies. With a 24-year age difference, both cultivate a wealth of traditional and contemporary perspectives that is reflected in how they build bridges between brands and different worlds.

They have been working in digital marketing for 5 years, initially in the spirituality niche and now in multiple global niches.
